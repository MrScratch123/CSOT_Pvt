### Input.GetKey is not working
Unity Editor by default uses its new input system. Since it is out of the scope of this course, we will switch to the old one. Here are the steps - 
* In the top left of the Unity Editor -> click on 'Edit'.
* The third bottom option is project settings. Open the 'Project Settings'.
* Then on the left of the new window you have different categories of project settings. They are alphabetically arranged. Find 'Player' in them. Click on the option to open those settings.
* On the right in the player settings you will find a drop down menu for 'Other settings'.
* Open that and scroll down to find the option 'Active Input Handling'.
* Click on the dropdown and select 'Both'. It will ask you to restart the editor,
* Click on apply and in 5 seconds it will automatically restart. Once it does the error should be gone.
