
# Week 1:

## The Game:
You will make a 3D game based on dodging enemy cubes and maximizing your score complete with a full game loop. 
For this you will be following the following playlist to get started. This will teach you the very basics of game development on Unity. This is a very complete and thorough course but is also rather fast paced course so feel free to pause, rewatch and look up things you dont understand on the Unity Documentation. Also get familiar with Brackeys he is gonne be your best friend in your unity learning journey.

# The Challenges:
* Replace the player cube with a 3D model of a car and the other things with rocks. Or spaceships. Or anything you can imagine.
* Add audio sfx to the game for crashing, game start, end etc.
* Make the game a single level, and make the whole game endless. Meaning you drive the cube and doge the other cubes endlessly in a straight line.
